## Standup Tips

### For Team Members
- **Be concise:** 2-3 minutes max
- **Be honest:** Share blockers early
- **Be positive:** Celebrate wins!
- **Be prepared:** Think before standup

### For Team Lead
- **Keep time:** Use a timer
- **Take notes:** Track blockers
- **Be supportive:** Help remove obstacles
- **Be energizing:** Start the day right!

### Red Flags to Watch For
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Same blocker 2 days in a row
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Team member with nothing to report
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Scope creep ("I'm also working on...")
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Integration issues between squads
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Low energy or morale
